In my senior year of high school, I began to struggle keeping up with my school work, I lacked motivation to go in everyday, I was barely getting any sleep, and experienced many anxiety attacks trying to cope with everything. I had not known that these were some of many symptoms of depression. Since then, I've found that I wasn't the only one going through these feelings and that many people had been confronting with these feelings with poor coping mechanisms. So I created Mom's House, to help my peers overcome their mental health issues and build a community to communicate with others to let them know that things aren't always fine and that's okay. We just have to learn how to manage.
